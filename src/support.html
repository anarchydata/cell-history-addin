Cell History – Support

Cell History is an Excel add-in that remembers where you've been in a workbook and lets you jump back with one click.

How it works

  • Stay on a cell for 5 seconds (configurable) and it is recorded in your history.
  • Moving quickly between cells does not create entries; staying on the same cell refreshes its timestamp instead of duplicating it.
  • Use the Back button (Home tab or task pane) to return to your previous location. Forward retraces your steps.
  • Click any entry in the history pane to jump straight to it, including across sheets.
  • History is stored inside the workbook, so it is restored when you reopen the file.

Frequently asked questions

Where is my data stored? Entirely inside your workbook file, using Office document settings. Nothing is sent to any server.

Why isn't a cell being recorded? The cursor must stay on the location for the full dwell time (default 5 seconds). You can lower it in the pane.

How do I clear the history? Use the Clear button at the top of the task pane, then save the workbook.

Privacy

Cell History does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data or workbook contents outside of the workbook itself. See the privacy policy.

NOTES FOR CERTIFICATION — Cell History

No account, login, or external service is required. The add-in works entirely inside Excel.

TEST STEPS
1. Open Excel (Windows desktop or Excel on the web).
2. Install / open the Cell History add-in.
3. On the Home tab, open the Cell History group and click Show History to open the task pane.
4. Click cell A1 and wait about 5 seconds. Confirm an entry appears in the history list (or the countdown completes and records it).
5. Click cell D10 (or another distant cell) and wait about 5 seconds so a second location is recorded.
6. Click Back on the ribbon (or Back in the pane). Confirm the selection returns to the previous location.
7. Click Forward in the pane. Confirm it moves forward again.
8. Click an older entry in the history list. Confirm Excel jumps to that sheet and address.
9. Optionally change the dwell seconds field, Clear history, then save and reopen the workbook to confirm history persistence after save.

EXPECTED RESULTS
- Locations are only recorded after the dwell time.
- Back / Forward and list clicks navigate correctly across sheets when applicable.
- No crashes; no external network calls required for core functionality.

SAMPLE DATA
None required. Any blank workbook is fine.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 21, 2026

Cell History is an Excel add-in that runs entirely within Microsoft Excel on your device.

Data collection

Cell History does not collect, transmit, or share any data. Specifically:

  • No personal information is collected.
  • No workbook contents, cell values, or cell locations are sent to any server.
  • No analytics, telemetry, or tracking of any kind is used.

Data storage

The history of cell locations you visit is stored exclusively inside your own workbook file using the standard Office document settings mechanism. This data never leaves your workbook and is under your control: clearing the history in the task pane and saving the workbook removes it.
